Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Text Clean Tools handles information when you use our website and browser-based text tools.

Text entered into our tools

The text you type or paste into Text Clean Tools is processed locally in your browser using client-side JavaScript. The contents of your text are not sent to our servers. We do not collect, view, store, sell, rent, or share the text you enter into our tools.

Advertising and cookies

This website may display ads from third-party advertising partners, including Google. These partners may use cookies or similar technologies to serve, personalize, and measure ads.

Advertising cookies do not provide Text Clean Tools or our advertising partners with access to the text you type or paste into our tools. The text-processing tools run separately in your browser, and the content entered into those tools is not shared with advertising providers by Text Clean Tools.

Hosting and basic technical information

Like most websites, our hosting and infrastructure providers may process basic technical information needed to deliver the site, such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages requested, and timestamps. This information is separate from the text you paste into the tools.

Links to other websites

Our site may contain links to third-party websites or advertisements. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of third-party websites.

Your choices

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. You may also use tools provided by advertising platforms to manage personalized advertising preferences.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to textcleantools@gmail.com.
