Frequently asked questions

Does this remove spaces inside normal sentences?

No. It removes lines that are empty or contain only whitespace.

Does it preserve line order?

Yes. Non-empty lines stay in their original order.

Is this private?

Yes. The tool runs in your browser and does not send pasted text to Text Clean Tools servers.
