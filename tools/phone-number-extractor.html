Frequently asked questions

What phone formats are supported?

The tool supports common US-style formats such as 555-123-4567, (555) 123-4567, and similar variations with spaces or dots.

Is my pasted text stored?

No. Your text is processed directly in your browser and is not collected, transmitted, or stored by Text Clean Tools.

Should I paste highly sensitive data?

No. The tools are designed for privacy, but you should avoid pasting passwords, private keys, medical records, financial account numbers, or highly sensitive personal data into any website.
